# Floating-Point Numbers

!SLIDE commandline incremental

	$ 5/3.
	1.66667

	$ 4/2.
	2.00000

	$ 5 div 3.
	1

	$ 5 rem 3.
	2

	$ 4 div 2.
	2

	$ Pi = 3.14159.
	3.14159

	$ R = 5.
	5

	$ Pi * R * R.
	78.5397

!SLIDE bullets transition=uncover

* `/` always returns a float

* `div` and `rem` are used for integer division and remainder
